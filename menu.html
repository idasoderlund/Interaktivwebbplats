MENU

Appetizer:

A exquisite selection of our appetizers, perfect for starting your meal.

  • Mediterranean Mezze Platter - served with cherry tomatoes, a drizzle of olive oil & warm pita bread
  • Japanese Gyoza - served with soy sauce, carrots & sesame oil
  • Mexican Elote (Street Corn) - served with BBQ dip & cilantro
  • Samosas - served with a tangy tamarind chutney & mint yoghurt sauce

Main Course:

Our main courses are prepared with the finest ingredients and love.

  • Risotto alla Milanese - served with a fresh salad
  • Injera with Doro Wat - served with hard boiled eggs
  • Butter Chicken - served with naan bread
  • Lasagna - served with ketchup & a fresh salad

Dessert:

Finish your meal with one of our delicious desserts.

  • Cannoli - tube-shaped shells filled with a sweet ricotta cheese mixture, often studded with chocolate chips or candied fruit
  • Gulab Jamun - deep-fried dough balls made from milk solids, served with a sweet syrup flavored with rosewater & cardamom
  • Kue Cubir - deep-fried dough pastry filled with sweetened coconut or chocolate
  • Sesame Balls (Jian Dui) - served with red bean paste
